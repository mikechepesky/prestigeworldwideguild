  • Welcome!

    This is the new website for Prestige Wörldwide, a World of Warcraft Horde guild on the US-Thunderhorn realm. This site includes general information about our guild, as well as our guild activities and raids, the roster, and more.

    A few resources are also provided, which might help you learn more about your class and role, the raid encounters that we're working on, or about WoW in general.

    FAQ
    Meet the officers
    • What realm do you play on?

      Our guild plays on US-Thunderhorn.

      What faction do you represent?

      For the Horde!

      How do you make that funny letter O in your guild name?

      Type Alt + 148.

      What types of activities do you do?

      We have members who are interested in nearly all of the things that WoW has to offer. We run weekly raids, and are currently working on filling up a second raiding group. We have members who love PvP, whether that's arenas, battlegrounds, or even world PvP. We do dungeons regularly, in many cases on multiple alts, and some of us even enjoy the occasional transmog or achievement run. Whatever you're interested in doing in-game, chances are there'll be someone around to join you.

      When and how often do you raid?

      We currently raid a minimum of twice a week, on Tuesdays and Thursdays at 8pm server time. If there's enough interest, we sometimes add Wednesdays or Sundays to the schedule. You can always find out when raids are scheduled by checking the in-game calendar.

      To learn more about our raids, check out the Guild Rules section.

      Do you use voice chat software?

      We use TeamSpeak 3 as our software of choice. You don't need to join the guild channel if you don't want to, but it can be a good way to get to know our members. We do consider it mandatory that you join TeamSpeak when raiding, however. You don't need to speak, or even have a mic, but it's much easier to coordinate raiding strategies when everyone can hear the raid leader's instructions.

      How do I join the guild?

      Simply whisper any of the guild members in-game, and we can send you an invite. If you have any other questions about the guild, its members, or our activities, feel free to message an officer. Click the Meet the officers button at the top of this page to see whom to contact.
